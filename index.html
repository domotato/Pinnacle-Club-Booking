📖 How to Book / 如何预订
  1. Check which time slot you'd like to join / 查看您想参加的时间段
  2. Tap the slot to select it / 点击该时间段以选择
  3. Enter your name and select your category (Male 18+ or Female/Male 18 & under) / 输入您的姓名并选择您的类别（18岁以上男性 或 女性/18岁及以下男性）
  4. Enter your contact number / 输入您的联系电话
  5. Tap Confirm Booking / 点击 Confirm Booking
That's it! You'll see your name appear on the list once confirmed. / 完成！预订成功后，您的名字将出现在名单上。
⚠️ This booking system is for repeat visitors only. If you are a first-timer, you must contact Ms Ji Ling (📞 9653 8069) to register before booking. Any booking under a name we do not recognise will be deleted without notice. If you are booking on behalf of a friend, please enter their name as "[Friend's name] - [Your name]".
⚠️ 本预订系统仅限回头访客使用。如果您是第一次参加，必须先联系 Ms Ji Ling（📞 9653 8069）进行登记，然后再预订。任何我们不认识的名字将被直接删除，恕不另行通知。如果您是帮朋友预订，请将名字输入为"[朋友的名字] - [您的名字]"。
To cancel your booking / 如需取消预订, please contact Ms Ji Ling (📞 9653 8069) or any Pinnacle Club admin before 12pm on game day. Any cancellation after this time is subject to review and may affect your ability to book future sessions. / 请联系 Ms Ji Ling（📞 9653 8069）或任何 Pinnacle Club 管理员，在活动当天中午12点之前取消。任何在此之后的取消将受到审查，并可能影响您未来的预订资格。
For any enquiries, please contact Ms Ji Ling: 📞 9653 8069 / 如有任何疑问，请联系 Ms Ji Ling：📞 9653 8069
